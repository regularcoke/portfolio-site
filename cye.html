CYE, 2022. html, javascript.

CYE was created to promote accessible photo based art through the use of webcams. The user is prompted to explore the possibilities of themselves and their environment to perform in front of their computer webcam. The final composition of this faux website is left to the user to create by uploading their unedited images captured on their webcam to their personal online gallery.
